About Me

Hello and welcome to my digital portfolio! My name is Jabria Roscoe and I'm an aspiring writer and journalist with a passion for helping others through my work.

I'm a recent graduate from the University of Miami (UM) where I recieved a Bachelor's Degree in Journalism.

During my time at UM I published articles for The Miami Hurricane, School of Communication, and the award-winning student run magazine, Distraction.

I am currently seeking full time employment in the marketing or journalism field. My goal is to work for a company that will allow me to make a positive contribution to my community.

In addition, I would like to continue to develop my writing and interpersonal skills through my work as a professional in the communications field.
